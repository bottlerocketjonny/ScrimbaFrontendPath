Visit Richmond

Standing proud at the gateway to the Yorkshire Dales, Richmond is one of Britain's most beautiful and vibrant market towns.

Top three activities to do in Richmond

Visit Richmond Castle

Take a tour around the best preserved Norman castle in England.

Check out a film at the Station Cinema

Watch the latest flick or grab a bite to eat in this converted train station.

Take a stroll in the Yorkshire Dales

Richmond is surrounded by a whole host of gorgeous country walks.
